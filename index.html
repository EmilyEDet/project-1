Grooming Station

Our team of pleasant and caring professionals is dedicated to keeping both you and your pet satisfied – it's our top priority! We strive to ensure your dog looks exactly the way you want.

Need an early drop off or late pick up time? Take advantage of our doggy daycare services, where our experienced Pack Leaders will look after your pup until you're ready to pick up – ensuring a hassle-free experience for both you and your furry friend.

Before booking a grooming appointment, make sure your dog meet our requirements. They must:

  • Be between 12 weeks - 12 years old
  • Be up to date on their Rabies vaccine (if 18 weeks or older)
  • Not be pregnant or in heat during their appointment
  • Be able to stand for extended periods of time
